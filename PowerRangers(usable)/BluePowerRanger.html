The Blue Power Ranger is usually the second in command of the "Mighty
Power Rangers". The Blue Ranger in earlier series were very serious
and known to be the smart Ranger on the battlefield. As the television
series went on, the Blue Ranger became a comic relief. Usually the Ranger
is a male but recnet series have introduced female charcters playing the
role with a faintly lighter blue suit or Power Armor.
