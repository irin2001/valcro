The Gold Power Ranger is a very powerful fighter and thinker.
He is always added to the show later in the season to help overcome a
a challenge the other Rangers are having trouble with. The original Gold
Rangers name was Trey of Triforia. He had three personalities: Wisdom, Heart,
and Courage which were ultimatly portrayed by identical triplets Tom, Tim,
and Ted DiFillippo.

    The list of the Gold Ranger characters include

  • Ted DiFilippo
  • Tom DiFilippo
  • Tim DiFilippo
  • Austin St. John
  • Paul F. Rosenthal (voice)
