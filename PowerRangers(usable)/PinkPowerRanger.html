The Pink Power Ranger is always given to one member on the Ranger
Squad. They are typically always female and follow other Rangers' advice.
They are given the most straight-forward weapons such as generic swords or
lances. Whenever a Pink Ranger is introduced into the series, they are always
accompanied with another female ranger.

    The list of the Pink Ranger characters include

  • Kimberly Ann Hart
  • Katherine Hillard
  • Cassie Chan
  • Kendrix Morgan
  • Dana Mitchell
  • Sydney Drew
  • Emma Goodall
  • Sarah Thompson
  • Karone
