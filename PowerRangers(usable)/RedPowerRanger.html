The Red Ranger is never to be messed with. He is usually the leader of
the Power Rangers. He is the "Head Honco", "Large and in Charge" He tells
the other Rangers what to do and they do it. He is strong and viginant but
has a shorter patience than the rest of the Rangers. In the Television show
Red Power Rangers are either the leader or a hot-headed rookie that was
recruited. They are always given a strong (sometimes the strongest) weapons
of all the Rangers.
